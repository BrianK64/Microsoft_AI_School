Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g of pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

Directions:

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water over medium-high heat.
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions (usually 8-10 minutes). Stir occasionally.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant (about 1-2 minutes).
  5. Pour the tomato sauce into the skillet with the garlic, stirring to combine. Let it simmer for 5 minutes, adding salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Once the pasta is done, drain it and add it to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Stir to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Serve the pasta on plates, and optionally, top with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade pasta!
