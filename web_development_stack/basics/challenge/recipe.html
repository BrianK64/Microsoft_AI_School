Margheritta Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Prepare the tomato sauce.
  2. Prepare the dough.
  3. Spread half a cup of the tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese
  5. Place the pizza in a 500oF preheated oven and bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and top it with fresh basil leaves
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza for a rich, fruity flavor.
  8. SLICE AND SERVE!
